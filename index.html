Direct services we deliver

Hands-on, on-site packages to improve flow, safety, and output.

Site Readiness Review

Promise: day one goes smoothly.

  • Punch list with owners and dates
  • 2-week fix plan
  • Photo notes for quick action

Potential cost savings: ~0.5 to 2% of project value from avoided delays and rework; often 1 to 3 days recovered on startup.

Logistics and Access Plan

Promise: crews and materials flow without choke points.

  • Site layout diagram
  • Delivery and egress routes
  • Laydown and staging map

Potential cost savings: ~1 to 3% on direct labor and equipment from less idle time and double handling.

Daily Production & Material Flow System

Promise: predictable output and on-time materials.

  • Simple live procurement tracker
  • Call-ahead cadence and late-risk board at the trailer
  • Daily goals sheet and end-of-day huddle script
  • Whiteboard or simple digital production log

Potential cost savings: ~1 to 5% via 5 to 15% productivity lift, fewer stoppages, and reduced standby charges.

Schedule Recovery Sprint

Promise: claw back lost days fast.

  • 2 to 4 week lookahead
  • Crew-by-crew plan
  • Constraint removal list

Potential cost savings: ~2 to 6% on labor and equipment or 5 to 15 days recovered on a 6-month job.

Subcontractor Onboarding

Promise: clean handoffs and fewer scope gaps.

  • Start-up package
  • Kickoff checklist
  • RFI and decisions lane rules

Potential cost savings: ~0.5 to 2% through reduced rework, back charges, and disputes.

Field Safety Package

Promise: fewer stoppages and tighter documentation.

  • Task-based SWPs
  • Toolbox talk calendar
  • Simple inspection forms fit to the work

Potential cost savings: ~0.5 to 3% from avoided stand-downs, administrative penalties, and lost time.

Traffic and Pedestrian Management

Promise: compliant, safe interfaces that keep work moving.

  • TCP sketches
  • Signage list
  • Staging and changeover sequence

Potential cost savings: ~0.5 to 2% from fewer shutdowns, fewer near misses, and less rework at interfaces.

Utility Locate and Permit Coordination

Promise: fewer hits and fewer permit surprises.

  • Ontario One Call log
  • Permit matrix
  • Constraint board in the trailer

Potential cost savings: ~1 to 5% through avoided damages, downtime, and emergency responses.

Incident Investigation and Response

Promise: contain impact fast and prevent repeat events.

  • Rapid site response and stabilization support
  • 24 to 48-hour factual report with root cause and corrective actions
  • Toolbox follow-up; guidance on WSIB and MOL notifications as needed

Potential cost savings: ~0.5 to 4% depending on severity, via reduced downtime, cleaner documentation, and prevention of repeat incidents.
